### Key Insight
miragejs-orm is the pragmatic migration path for Studio's mock layer. Same ORM concepts (models, factories, relationships, serializers) rebuilt in TypeScript — our 50 models map 1:1 and the built-in serializers replace our 4 `@ts-ignore`'d custom ones. Pairing it with MSW cleanly separates ORM from request interception, which is why the `handleFromCypress` hack exists in the first place.
