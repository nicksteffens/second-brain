### What We Accomplished
- Reviewed v4 migration guide from UI repo (`docs/migration-v4.md`)

- Migrated `ExploreTactics.tsx` — converted `cardMedia`, `cardHeader`, `galleryContent` to `InkCardMedia`, `InkCardHeader`, `InkCardContent`, `InkCardActions` subcomponents
