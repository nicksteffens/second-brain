### What We Accomplished
- Audited all GitHub Actions CI runs for `studio-push.yml` and `canvas-push.yml` since March 16
- Counted 442 Percy snapshots per studio run, 683 per canvas run across 163+ test files
- Found 297 studio CI runs and 76 canvas CI runs — totaling ~183k estimated snapshots in 11 days
- Built per-branch breakdown showing `ec/company-based-routes` (47k snapshots) and `feature/sc-189978/provider-name` (15.7k) as top consumers
- Identified 6 cross-package branches that triggered both suites, burning ~73k snapshots alone
- Added full audit findings with data tables to the Vizzly research Notion doc to strengthen the migration case

### Challenges Encountered
- None significant

### Most Valuable Collaboration
Speed of investigation — went from "why are we burning snapshots?" to a full per-branch breakdown with concrete numbers in under 20 minutes, then documented it directly in Notion.

### Key Insight
Cross-package PRs are the real cost driver. Branches touching both `packages/studio` and `packages/canvas` fire ~1,125 snapshots per push (442 + 683). With Percy's per-snapshot billing, team velocity directly increases costs — the more productive the team, the more they pay.
