### Most Valuable Collaboration
Semver incident triage — quickly diagnosing why release-it didn't detect the breaking change (parses individual commits, not merge commit bodies) and establishing the fix in one pass.

### Key Insight
release-it with conventional-changelog parses **individual commit messages** for version determination. The merge commit body containing `BREAKING CHANGE:` is not sufficient — the actual commits on the branch must use `feat!:` or include a `BREAKING CHANGE:` footer. Without documentation, contributors have no way to know this.
