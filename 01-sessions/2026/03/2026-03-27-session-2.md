### Most Valuable Collaboration
CI debugging — tracing the `hashFiles` timeout, comparing against other branches and the previous commit to confirm it was infrastructure contention rather than our change.

### Key Insight
`hashFiles('**/yarn.lock')` is a hidden performance bomb. The recursive glob is functionally identical to `hashFiles('yarn.lock')` since only the root lockfile matters (63 others are stale files in gitignored `node_modules/`), but the `**` causes 120s timeouts when multiple workflows compete for runner resources.
